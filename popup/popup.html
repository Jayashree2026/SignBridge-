Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a room?

Click "Create Room" to generate a unique room code. Share this code with others who want to join your meeting.

How do I join a room?

Click "Join Room" and enter the room code provided by the signer.

What permissions are needed?

The extension needs camera access for sign language recognition and microphone access for speech-to-text functionality.

How do I save the transcript?

Transcripts are automatically saved when you end a meeting. You can access them from the Records page.
